TOWER of HANOI

MOVES: 0

Objective

The objective of the Tower of Hanoi is to move all the rings from the first pole to the second or third pole, following these rules:

  • Only one ring can be moved at a time.
  • Each move consists of taking the upper ring from one of the stacks and placing it on top of another stack or on an empty pole.
  • A larger ring cannot be placed on top of a smaller ring.
